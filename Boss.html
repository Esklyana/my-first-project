Careers

We’re constantly on the look out for great people.

We have great hang-out spots in downtown Washington DC, Paris, and Saint Petersburg where the magic happens, but most of our team members choose to travel the world and work remotely.

Here are just a few of the projects you could be a part of:

  • Work on real-time and historical aggregation and analysis for financial, blockchain, and natural language data.
  • Develop crypto-native financial instruments.
  • Contribute to open-source projects that take artificial intelligence and natural language processing to the next level.

Be a part of something big

You'll join a team of computer engineers, security experts, scientists, mathematicians, and finance experts from the US, Europe, and Russia. You'll get to work on cutting edge technologies in IT security, blockchain, and finance.

Enjoy your work

Flexible work hours and flexible location. We have a clear and open development process on GitLab. You'll see the results of your work with zero red tape slowing things down. Asynchronous nature of GitLab allows us to accommodate any time zone, work schedule, and side gigs of our team members.

Who we need

Proactive self-starters who can work with little oversight. Our projects are highly technical, so we expect you to have scientific background and relevant experience in IT security, data analysis, programming, blockchain technologies, financial risk management, securities trading, cryptocurrency, or any other related area.

General Requirements

  • Fluent English. We are an international team and most of the business is conducted in English. You need to be able to speak and write fluently.
  • Background in IT, Mathematics, Finance, or other sciences.
  • We don't discriminate based on race, nationality, age, sex, sexual orientation, physical abilities, or political views. We expect the same from you.

Whether you fit any of the profiles listed Below or not, send us your resume at careers@incas.ec.
